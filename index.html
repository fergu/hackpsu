  • PATIENT SAFETY:

    We practice strict sterilization protocal as recommended under IDA guidelines. We autoclave (steam sterilize) our hand pieces (drill) and all other instruments. Those few items, which cannot be autoclaved are disposed off. all surfaces in the treatment room are cleaned and disinfected. We follow a four tier sterilization protocol


    All the instruments are scrubbed thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol swabs to remove any attached debris.




    Antiseptic dip: After use the instruments from each patient are saturated solution of an antiseptic for at least 12 hours.




    Dried instruments are then packed in sterilization pouches (eco friendly reusable and not plastic) and sterilized using an Autoclave. This is a high pressured steam chamber where instruments are placed at a specified temperature to kill all the micro organisms including their spores.
